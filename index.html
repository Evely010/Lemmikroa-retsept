Lemmikroa retsept

Ingredient Amount
Chicken breast 400 g
Santa Maria Tom Kha soup seasoning spice mix 30 g
Water 300 ml
Coconut milk 500 ml
Champignons 100 g
Cherry tomatoes 100 g
  1. Mix Tom Kha soup seasoning spice mix with water and coconut milk.
  2. Heat until boiling.
  3. Slice chicken breast and boil for 5 minutes.
  4. Add chopped cherry tomatoes and champignons.
